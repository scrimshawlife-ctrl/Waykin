## Current milestone: M2

M2 succeeds when a recognizable procedural Lira can be placed, replaced, cleared, and switched among five visually distinct deterministic states from the AR Lab.

## M2 physical exit evidence

- camera feed opens
- tracking becomes active
- Place Lira creates a stable companion
- second placement replaces the prior companion
- idle/follow/investigate/alert/celebrate are distinguishable
- discovery and threat placeholders render
- clear removes all registered entities
- background/foreground behavior is recorded
- no regression claim is made for the walking product without running its suite
