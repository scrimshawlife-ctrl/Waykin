**Protective contract before movement sessions.**  
Shown at first run and available anytime under Settings → Legal → Safety.

## Please remember

1. **Stay aware of traffic, terrain, weather, and other people.** Audio and AR must never replace looking up.
2. **Use a safe volume.** Keep enough hearing free for horns, bikes, and voices.
3. **You can pause or end anytime.** Stopping is protective — never a failure or punishment.
4. **Hunt / pressure modes are controlled fiction.** Lira’s “pressure” language is intentional entertainment, not real threat or violence.
5. **AR is optional.** If the world is busy or light is harsh, skip AR and stay in audio/session chrome.
6. **Do not use while driving or operating machinery.**
7. **Respect private property and local laws.** Route suggestions (if any) are guides, not permission to trespass.
8. **Health readings (if enabled) are not medical care.** They do not detect emergencies.

## If something feels wrong

Pause or end the session, put the phone away, and handle the real-world situation first.

## Accessibility

- System **Reduce Motion** is respected for non-essential loops.
- Critical controls (Pause / End) remain available mid-session.
